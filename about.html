About Jules

Jules is an internationally recognized scholar, innovator, entrepreneurial leader, web designer, web developer, and video producer committed to sparking inspiration, crafting visuals, and forging connections among people to bring joy.

An entrepreneurial leader, Jules has been using his passions and personal stories to inspire people about the importance of adopting an entrepreneurial thinking approach to solving daily problems. He has an extensive list of widely recognized stories, including the famous article on defining and validating problems in Human-Centered Design, Inside the Mind of a Problem Solver. Besides that, Jules has been inspiring readers with his content through his published story on a successful entrepreneurial transformation of his community through farming chickens.

As an innovator, Jules created a widely celebrated creativity mission statement summarizing his day-to-day practices and values. The Enable it - Model it - Embrace it, mission statement which he developed at the end of his foundational entrepreneurship class at Princeton University, provides a futuristic approach to encourage creativity on a personal level and in others.

    Enable it — Ask open-ended questions

    Model it — Try new experiences and look for new ways of seeing things

    Embrace it — Always Learn, build, and Act.

Formally educated as a pre-medicine student, Jules changed his imagined career path during the COVID-19 pandemic lockdown to explore emerging technologies. This pushed him to study basic web development languages and designing tools that he uses in his part-time web designing and web development occupations. His first published website became a portal for students to get entrepreneurship courses and learn about digital solution development during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Jules as an internationally recognized scholar is a student at Princeton University from Rwanda in the Department of Computer Science through a Bachelor of Science in Engineering Program. His research interests include Uniting African Descents, the Impacts of Genetic Ancestry Tests on Consumers, and the Development of Countries in terms of Adopting Emerging Technologies (Titles: Singapore and the Dawn of Lab-grown Meat; Rwanda and the Rise of mRNA Vaccines in Africa).

Jules combines his passion for inspiring and connecting people with creating videos. He believes video production stands out as a powerful medium for capturing attention, engaging audiences, and conveying messages effectively. Therefore, Jules takes advantage of video production because of its dynamic blend of visuals, motion, sound, and storytelling to evoke emotions, tell compelling narratives, and leave a lasting impact.
